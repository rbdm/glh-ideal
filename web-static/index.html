A Computational Representation of a Legal Case


The Ideal Legal Development Environment allows our clients to build a computational representation of their legal analysis to power a suite of tools designed to enhance, simplify and empower their workflow without loss of information.

Build your legal analysis into native machine readable structures so that nothing is lost in translation.

Leverage intelligent assistance that understands your legal reasoning and workflow.

Export and share your legal reasoning with any audience without rewriting or redoing.

Generating the Representation


The flexiblity of the Computational Representation allows our clients to develop their legal analysis using a powerful, intuitive editor which supports unique and exciting ways to input data, logical arguments and supplementary material.

Suggested edits, changes and updates extrapolated from a text, voice or video conversation with a client.

Upload existing legal documents and material to generate an initial representation of the case.

Collaborate with team members whilst maintaining version history, security and project integrity.

Transformation and Analysis


Our technology empowers our clients to transform and analyse the Computational Representation to provide assistive tools, automation and insights that improve the quality and efficiency of your work.

Extrapolate key insights, facts and information about your case to reduce noise and increase clarity.

Query and search your legal case to rapidly identify the information you need, as you need it.

Contribute your legal reasoning to your organisation's artificial intelligence to share and build knowledge amongst your team.
